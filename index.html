DEPENDENCE ON SOCIAL MEDIA

Introduction

Social media has become an integral part of modern life, connecting people across the globe and providing instant access to information and entertainment. However, excessive reliance on social media can lead to dependence, affecting various aspects of daily life. This website explores the factors contributing to social media dependence, its impact on individuals, and ways to mitigate its negative effects.

Factors of Dependence

  • Interesting Content: Algorithms designed to retain user attention.
  • Ease of Access: Continuous mobile usage.
  • Social Gratification: Acceptance through likes, comments, and followers.
  • FOMO: Fear of missing out on the latest developments.

Effects of Social Media Dependence

  • Social Relationships: Decreased face-to-face interaction with family and friends.
  • Productivity: Diminished concentration on work or studies.
